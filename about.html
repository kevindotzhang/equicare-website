Our Founder

Kaitlyn Zhang

Founder & CEO

Kaitlyn's passion for horses began in childhood and evolved into a mission to revolutionize equine healthcare through technology. As a data scientist with experience in machine learning and AI, she recognized the potential to combine cutting-edge algorithms with veterinary science to help horse owners make better informed decisions about their companions' health.

To revolutionize equine healthcare by making advanced AI and scientific research accessible to every horse owner, empowering them to make informed decisions that enhance their horses' health, happiness, and quality of life.
